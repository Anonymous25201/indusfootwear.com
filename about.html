About Us

Our History

Founded in 1999 in Khairthal, Rajasthan, by Mr. Mahesh Kumar Chachlani, Indus Footwear began with a focus on slipper distribution, starting with a small team and basic resources. Over time, we expanded our operations and adopted advanced machinery to improve our manufacturing capabilities.

In 2011, we introduced a new line of men's footwear, marking a key phase in our growth. We expanded internationally, particularly into the Middle East, while maintaining a strong presence in India.

Our Vision & Mission

Today, we serve markets in the Middle East, Africa, and India, with plans for further global expansion. We aim to become one of India's largest footwear brands, delivering high-quality, comfortable, and durable footwear.

We prioritize innovation, sustainable practices, and meeting diverse customer needs, all while continuing to push boundaries in the footwear industry.
